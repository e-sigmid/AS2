IM
IM is a vocational subject, you'll learn how to code and use coding languages. You also learn how to edit pictures, videos and how to take pictures/use a camera. you're only at the school for 2 years and then decide to go out to be a trainee for a year or choose something else. After the first year you decide to either go IT or Medie for the second year, there are only 30 places for IT and 15 for Medie but AS is the only school in Norway with Medie in the second year, which means people from different schools can come and take your place.
Amalie Skram
Amalie Skram was founded in 2014 in honor of the writer Amalie Skram, the school is located in Bergen. The school is huge, at the school there is AdO named after the swimmer Alexander Dale Oen and it has 50 meters pools to swim in. + a bench worth 2 million kr.
